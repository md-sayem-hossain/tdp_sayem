Observation 2 (Day Two)

Date: 01/06/2024 10.00AM - 11.00AM
Vanue: D8.12

Overall Description

The class was fantastic, with students fully engaged and the teacher leading an informative and interesting session. The lecturer took the time to explain how the course would unfold, including details about upcoming assignments and how we would tackle them. Everyone seemed excited about the course content and eager to get started. The teacher's clear explanations and approachable demeanor set a positive tone for the rest of the term, leaving everyone feeling confident and motivated about what lies ahead.

Strength

The class had excellent student engagement, with everyone actively participating and showing real interest in the lesson. The teacher's explanation of the course structure and assignments was clear and helpful, setting a positive tone for the term.

Weekness

There was no weekness seen. Overall the session was good
